ABOUT ME

Grown in London, I come from an academic background in cognitive psychology by way of UL. I spent some time working in independent film acquisitions during those years but immediately gravitated towards startups when I discovered design.

I moved to San Francisco early in my career to join a founding team exploring tools for creativity, where I designed Cassette. There I discovered design thinking and made it a core philosophy to drive my design process, working alongside a CEO from the d.school and advising from David Kelley.

Outside of my work, I place a high value on creativity, learning and relationships. As a lifelong musician I’m always consuming music. These days travel, mindfulness, food, art, architecture, and a healthy dose of matcha and leisure sports (currently: tennis) keep me going.
